Barking Love is a company that offers Pet Services in Toronto. Being a dog lover myself, I was very excited to take on the challenge to redesign their brand and create a clean and easy-to-use website that will be launched early this summer.

My Role

Branding and Web Designer

Goal

To redesign Barking Love's brand and create the look for their first website.

Type & Duration

Freelance - Ongoing

About Barking Love

Ana Andrade, AKA Barking Love, is a dog specialist in Toronto, ON, Canada. A Veterinary MD from Brazil, she is a dog lover and runs the company with passion and commitment. Barking Love offers pet sitting, live-in care, dog boarding, dog walking, and orientation in a series of topics such as adoption, husbandry, and natural therapeutic feeding for dogs.

Brand Promise

We are a trustful service that delivers pet parents peace of mind while away from their furry loved ones. We provide dogs with a personalized, fun and safe experience grounded in love and care.

Challenge & Solution

On top of having to clearly speak about dogs, Barking Love’s overall branding needs to reflect the keywords that represent their personality and values: Trust, Fun, Love & Care.

As the final logo, we chose a pictorial mascot which, along with the colour, sums up all the main concepts the brand represents.

We felt that the truest way to portray Love and Care, the epicenter of the brand’s values, was to fuse a heart as a feature of the mascot itself. The orange line drawing makes the logo approachable and Fun.

Using the Golden Ratio to design a shape that would be proportionate and symmetric was a way of inspiring professionalism and Trust.

In addition to the logo, the friendly sans-serif typography, the light UI Kit and the heartfelt photography style add to portraying Barking Love’s values throughout the brand.
