Thunderwear

UX Research & Design

Personal Project

Thunderwear is a weather app that focuses on optimizing a user’s morning routine and minimizing the amount of decisions they have to make by showing them what clothing & accessories they should wear or carry when leaving home.


This is a project that started as a simple Javascript assignment, but I believed to be interesting enough to be backed up with a full User Research process.

Hypothesis

If users can find out what clothing & accessories they are supposed to wear in an easy and intuitive way, their morning routine will be improved by minimizing decision making. Thus, subtracting from the users’ amount of daily cumulative non-heuristic decisions that result in decision fatigue and ensuring they are properly dressed to face the elements.
