Henrique Salvador

Product Designer

I am a multidisciplinary UX Designer creating user-centric cutting-edge solutions to multi-billion dollar brands in finance and retail at Solvers.

Before joining Solvers, I worked as a UX Researcher and Designer for 2 years in a cross-industry academic research lab helping small and medium-sized businesses in Ontario uncover user needs and business opportunities.

Formerly, as a UX Developer, I was part of highly collaborative self-managed teams at Ateliê de Software, a pioneer in Lean UX and Agile development in South America, where I led and executed many creative initiatives – from the conceptualization of SaaS for start-ups to large-scale platform redesigns in the governmental business.
